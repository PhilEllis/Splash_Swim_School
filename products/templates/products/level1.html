Seahorse

Level 1 - Seahorse is suitable for those who are young and new to the water or those slightly older children who are not confident or unsure in the water. At this level our main swim teacher will have additional teachers in the water with them to help your child navigate this new watery world safely and with confidence.

Seahorse is for beginners and although we do have additional help in the water at this stage we also need your child to be able to do the following to help ensure we deliver a safe course. Your child’s feeling of safety in the water will directly correlate to their confidence in the water so it is important we get this right.

Listening Skills

Old enough to comprehend and follow simple instructions. This skill is super important, allowing them to follow the teachers instructions.

Some Strength

They have to be strong enough to pull themselves up onto the side of the pool. This is about safety first and a non negoitable.

Pool Experience

This can’t be their first ever time in a pool. It is best to get your child familiar with the environment of a pool before booking.
