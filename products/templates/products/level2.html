Level 2

Frog level 2 marks a significant leap in your child's swimming journey. Frog

Level 2 - Frog is ideal for children who have graduated from the Seahorse level. It's for those who are now confident in the water and can swim a few meters without the aid of floats. With fewer instructors in the water, children at this level start to develop greater autonomy.

Frog swimmers still need alot of support in the water and although we do have additional help in the water at this stage we also need your child to be able to do the following to help ensure we deliver a safe course. Your child’s feeling of safety in the water will directly correlate to their confidence in the water so it is important we get this right.

Confidence in Water

Display comfort and confidence in the water, swimming a couple of meters unaided.

Basic Swimming Skills

Have a basic understanding of swimming movements, ready to refine these skills.

Pool Familiarity

Be familiar with the pool environment and follow more complex instructions.
