Orca

Level 3 - Orca is tailored for swimmers who can confidently traverse the width of the pool unaided. This level emphasizes skill refinement and stroke variation.

Orca is for established swimmers and due to this they will have less support in the water so at this level your child needs to be able to do the following.

Swimming Proficiency

Have an established front crawl and able to swim a width of the pool unaided.

Stroke Development

Swimmers need to have basic knowledge of the additional swim strokes as the focus of this level it is all about focusing on refining different swimming strokes.

Increased Independence

Show increased independence in the water and follow complex swimming drills.
