Swim Levels

At Splash Swim School, we understand that every child is unique and progresses in their aquatic journey at their own pace. That's why we offer a range of swim levels designed to cater to various skill sets and ages, ensuring a tailored and effective learning experience for each child. From beginners taking their first strokes to advanced swimmers refining their technique, we have a level that fits every need.

Choosing the Right Swim Level for Your Child

Choosing the right swim level is essential to ensure your child's safety and progress. When booking, please make sure to choose the level that matches your child's ability and the location that works best for your family. Once you have created an account you will be invited to create a profile for your child providing us will all of their important information. This way, we can ensure that your child receives the most beneficial, safe and enjoyable swimming experience at Splash Swim School.

Splash Facts

Before we delve into the exciting facts about Splash Swim School, it's essential to understand the remarkable journey that brought us here. Our founders, with a rich background in teaching for a collective twenty years, embarked on a mission to create a swim school that would not only teach children how to swim but also instill a lifelong love for the water.

Having spent two decades honing their teaching skills, they brought a wealth of experience, expertise, and passion to the creation of Splash Swim School. Their vision was to provide a nurturing and safe environment where children could develop essential swimming skills, gain confidence, and ultimately become strong, proficient swimmers. This dedication to excellence and child-centered teaching forms the core of our values at Splash Swim School.
